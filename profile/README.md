# Flattered! And a sense of validation and accomplishment

Flattered! And a sense of validation and accomplishment. All my mods begin with an idea/design that I then try and turn into a mod people might like so to see CDPR officially implement something so close to my original idea, and people enjoying it, is very satisfying. It must have been a good idea.


You’ve made many mods that expand on the romance options in the game, who is your favourite and why? 

Judy! She’s just so sweet, too good for Night City. There’s something very genuine about her. She gives V a powerful reason to live (she does make it hard to pick any ending other than The Star though!) It is quite the achievement to be the only person making quest mods for Cyberpunk.
